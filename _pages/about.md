I’m currently a Ph.D. Student in Computer Science at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn), advised by [Minghui Zhou](https://minghuizhou.github.io). Before this, I graduated from [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn) in June 2024 and earned my Bachelor’s degree in Automatic Science. I’m interested in looking into the process of software development and explore fancy ways to improve it, as well as comprehensively understanding and improving the interaction between human and computer. Roughly speaking, my research interest includes human-centered computing, AI for SE, HCI, etc.
